Shuffly

Experience YouTube in a new way

What is it, and what can you do with it

Shuffly is the best place to discover new content on YouTube or just to chill and spend some time.
Shuffly is not a replacement for the YouTube app, they serve different purposes.
Sometimes YouTube can be a little overwhelming and Shuffly's minimal and straightforward design is thought to solve this problem.

YouTube

You can shuffle over the trending videos from YouTube or over a specific keyword.
You can also shuffle over a set of videos that you may like. Those videos are selected by Shuffly based on the liked videos from your YouTube account.

Channels

If you want more control over the video playback, you can add your favourite YouTube channels and shuffle over their Uploaded videos, Liked videos or Favourite videos.

Categories

Here you can shuffle over the most popular videos from all the YouTube video categories.
