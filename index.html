What is it, and what can you do with it

Shuffly is the best place to discover new cool stuff on YouTube, to chill or simply to watch some videos from your favourite YouTube channels.

Shuffly is not a replacement for the YouTube app, they serve different purposes.
Sometimes YouTube can be a little overwhelming, especially if you just want to chill or watch something to fill some free time.
Shuffly minimal and straightforward design will help you doing just that :)

Personalized

Shuffly knows you, if you just want to relax without having to worry about what to watch, pick the "videos you may like" option and let Shuffly do the work.
Otherwise you can shuffle over your personal playlists, such as: your uploads, your liked videos, you favourites and your "Watch later" playlist.

Adaptable

It's also possible to shuffle over a specific keyword or over the most popular videos on YouTube.

Channels and Playlists

If you want more control over the video playback, you can choose between your personal YouTube playlists and all the channels and playlists on YouTube.

Channels

Besides every channel's playlists, is also super easy to shuffle over the uploaded, liked and favourite videos of all YouTube channels.
